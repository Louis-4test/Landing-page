My UI/UX Design Services

High-fidelity Prototype

I create a prototype to demonstrate the behavior of the application and show how it would work in reality. I design fully-interactive prototypes to give you an idea of the workflow of your project.

Mobile and Web User Interface Design

I deliver my best efforts to ingeniously convert your ideas and thoughts into a clear and fully functional design. I design user interfaces that conform to what the user requires and understands.

Technical Design

I can also create a technical design that helps my clients understand the technical workflow of the project. In technical design, I define technology stack for all the components of the app, third-party integrations and interactions of services and APIs.

Wireframes

I design wireframes to provide you an outline of a mobile or web app by demonstrating content, structure and functionality that will exist on your app’s and software’s pages.

Cross-Platform Compatibility

Focusing on the core principles of UI design and functionality implementation, i design and deliver responsive and cross-platform compatible designs to ensure that it works across different platforms.

Software Redesign

If you already have an app or a website but it does not look appealing or professional, i can help you by re-designing the entire website or application. After evaluating your app and understanding the users, i make your software look engaging and beautiful.

My approach to providing UI/UX Design Services

Concept Presentation

UI/UX Wireframes

I prototype the structure of the entire app or website, revealing what should be present on the app pages.

Fully Functional UX/UI

Once the client approves wireframes, i create a fully functional UI/UX for your application.

Prototype Testing

Front-end Development

UI Testing Process
